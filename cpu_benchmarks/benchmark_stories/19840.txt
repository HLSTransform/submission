Once upon a time, there was a princess who had a special jewel. It was very pretty and sparkly. One day, the jewel fell off her crown and got lost. The princess was very sad and didn't know what to do. 
A kind man found the jewel and gifted it back to the princess. She was very happy and thanked the man. The princess wanted to restore the jewel to her crown, so she took it to the jeweler. 
The jeweler was able to restore the jewel and put it back on the princess's crown. Now, the princess was very happy and wore her crown with the beautiful jewel everywhere she went. The end.
