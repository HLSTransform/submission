Once upon a time there was a frog. He was very unhappy with how he looked. He wished he had a big house like his friends.
One day, the frog got very lucky. He went to a magic lab, and the scientist there changed him. He became bigger, and his skin turn green. He was so excited about his new look!
He now had a beautiful house just like his friends'. Everyone was jealous of him, but the frog didn't care. He was happy to have a house that he could call his own. 
He thanked the scientist at the lab, and hopped away to enjoy his new life. He had become so lucky, and he was never unhappy again.
