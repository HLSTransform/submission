Once there was a great woman. Every day she would send a good message to her friends. She wanted everyone to be happy and have a nice day. On a special day, the woman sent an even greater message. Everyone was so excited that they wanted to thank her. The woman gave them a big hug and a smile so wide that it almost touched her ears! Everyone was so happy and they loved the woman very much. They sent her lots of hugs and kisses back and it made her feel great.
