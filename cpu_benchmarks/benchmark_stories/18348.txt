Once upon a time, there was a colorful bird. The bird liked to pick things. One day, the bird saw a shiny thing on the ground. The bird did not know it was a weapon.
The bird wanted the shiny weapon. So, the bird went down to pick it up. The bird was very happy with the shiny weapon. The bird showed the weapon to all its friends. The friends were scared of the weapon, but the bird did not know why.
Then, one day, the bird tried to play with the weapon. The bird got hurt by the weapon. The bird's friends were sad. The bird did not play anymore. The end.
