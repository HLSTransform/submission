Molly was a little girl who wanted to explore the world. One day, she saw a rare umbrella lying in the garden. She was so excited that she decided to lift it up. Little did she know but the umbrella was magical. As soon as Molly lifted it up, it started to rain. Raindrops came down all over the place. Molly got a little scared but when she opened the umbrella, the rain stopped. She was so amazed that she lifted the umbrella up and twirled around with it. Suddenly, everywhere Molly went, the sun began to shine. Molly felt so proud and happy that she was able to make it happen. She never thought she could do something so special with a rare umbrella!
