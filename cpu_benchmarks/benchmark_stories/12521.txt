Once upon a time, there was a cute little bunny named Benny. Benny loved to eat apples. One day, he decided to invite his friends to a picnic in the park. He wanted to share his apples with them.
Benny hopped to his friend Timmy's house and invited him to the picnic. Timmy was excited and said yes. Then, Benny went to his friend Sally's house and invited her too. Sally was happy and said she would bring some carrots.
At the picnic, Benny and his friends ate apples and carrots. They played games and had fun. Benny was happy that he invited his friends to the picnic. He realized that sharing his apples made him feel good inside.
