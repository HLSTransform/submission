Once upon a time, there was a boy named Timmy. He loved playing with his cart, which was his favorite toy. One day, he wanted to be popular and have lots of friends to play with.
Timmy decided to make a lemonade stand with his cart. He worked hard and succeeded in making delicious lemonade. People loved it and soon he became very popular. Timmy was happy because he had lots of friends to play with now. 
From that day on, Timmy and his cart became famous in the neighborhood. They would sell lemonade every weekend and everyone loved it. Timmy was proud of his success and loved playing with his cart even more.
