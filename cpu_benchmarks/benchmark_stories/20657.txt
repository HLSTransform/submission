Once upon a time there was a regular pumpkin. Every day, the pumpkin would print the sun up in the sky and make all the flowers turn bright and happy. One day, the regular pumpkin wanted to learn something new.
The pumpkin went to the store and asked the shopkeeper for help. The shopkeeper gave the pumpkin a special printer. The pumpkin was so excited and took the printer home.
The pumpkin figured out how to print different things like pictures and words. Every day the pumpkin printed something new and colorful.
Soon, the pumpkin was a pro at printing. He printed pictures of his friends and printed funny stories for everyone to read. The regular pumpkin was so proud of what he could do.
The pumpkin would print up the sun everyday and make everyone smile. He was so happy that he learned how to print!
