Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Timmy. Timmy loved to eat yummy food. One day, he smelled something tasty coming from the kitchen. It was his mom's famous chocolate cake! Timmy couldn't wait to eat it.
Suddenly, the doorbell rang. Timmy's mom went to answer it and Timmy followed her. It was his grandma! She had come to visit. Timmy was so happy to see her. They all went to the kitchen to have some cake.
Timmy's mom cut a big slice of cake for everyone. Timmy took a bite and it was so tasty! He loved it. Then, his grandma told him to turn around and look outside. There was a beautiful rainbow in the sky. Timmy was amazed. It was a wonderful day!
