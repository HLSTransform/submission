Sleepy was a bear. He wanted to be friends with the birds, but the birds were not very nice to him. They said mean things and never welcomed him. Sleepy was sad until one day something amazing happened. 
The birds changed their mind about Sleepy and welcomed him. They said they were sorry and that they wanted to be friends. Sleepy couldn't believe it! He was so glad to be welcomed by the birds. 
The birds and Sleepy became friends and they spent a lot of time together. They played together in the garden and had lots of fun. Every time the birds said something mean to Sleepy, he always forgave them. 
Sleepy and the birds were very happy together and they were all glad that they chose to forgive each other and be friends.
