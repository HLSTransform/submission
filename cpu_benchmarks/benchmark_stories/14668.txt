Once upon a time, there was a person. This person was honest and kind. One day, the person was feeling sad and worried. They had a big problem they didn't know how to solve.
Then, the person decided to share their problem with a friend. The friend was understanding and kind. They listened to the person and told them what to do.
The friend said: "The only way out of this problem is to release it. Just let it go and trust that it will be solved." The person took the words of the friend and decided to try release.
So, the person said goodbye to worries and let the problem go. It was hard, but with the help of their friend, the person was able to let go.
The person felt good. They felt lighter and happier. With release, the problem was solved. Happiness spread from their heart and everyone around them felt it too.
