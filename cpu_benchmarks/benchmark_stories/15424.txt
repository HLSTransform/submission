Tim cheered as Daddy went up to the stage and showed off his special suit. He had worked very hard and was now getting recognition. His face was beaming with pride, and everyone was clapping and cheering.
When Daddy was finished, Tim hugged him tightly. His daddy had accomplished something amazing, and Tim was so proud. Daddy said he was ready for anything now in his special suit.
