Sam was a 3 year old child. He liked to read books. Every night before he went to bed, he read a story. One night he was worried. He had an enormous problem! He had to pick which book to read! Sam couldn't decide! There were so many books to choose from! In the end, he decided to read a book about a dinosaur. Sam read the book and when he finished, he felt happy. His worry was gone! He was ready to go to bed.
