Once upon a time there was a kind girl. She always tried to do the right thing, being honest and thoughtful. Her friends noticed and they respected her, too.
Everyday, the girl would bow as she said hello to her friends. She was so polite and always smiled.
Most days, the girl felt an urge to help her friends. She wanted to do something nice for them and make them happy.
The girl's friends started to notice her kind actions. They realized how much she cared about them, and returned the favor. They all found joy in helping each other.
And life was good! The girl and her friends were blessed with honesty and kindness for many years to come.
