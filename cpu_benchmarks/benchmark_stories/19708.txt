Once upon a time there was a dish. It was always very, very happy. It was the best dish in the world. One day, the dish just wanted to be filled up with something sweet. It was very excited. So, it looked around for something sweet and found some honey! The dish was very delighted and began to smile. It was so happy, it even did a little dance! The honey filled the dish so nicely, and it was just too sweet! The dish liked it so much that it stayed happy forever. The end.
