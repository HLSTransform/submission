Once upon a time, there was a dependable cat named Tom. Tom loved to eat cheese. Every day, he would go to the kitchen and find cheese to eat. Tom was very happy when he ate cheese.
One day, a big ball came into the room. Tom wanted to play with the ball. He ran and jumped to strike the ball. The ball went very far away. Tom was very good at striking the ball.
Tom played with the ball all day. When it was time to eat, he went to the kitchen. He found his cheese and ate it all up. Tom was a happy and dependable cat who loved cheese and playing with the ball.
