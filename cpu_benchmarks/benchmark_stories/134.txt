Once upon a time, there was a happy little girl named Lily. She loved playing outside and exploring the earth. One day, she found a big rock that she wanted to turn into a special present for her mommy. She thought and thought about how to design it just right.
But then, a naughty squirrel came along and tried to steal the rock! Lily was upset and scared. She didn't want to lose her special present for her mommy. But then, she remembered to be grateful for all the other rocks and pretty things she had found on earth. She decided to let the squirrel have the rock and be happy with what she had. And in the end, Lily still made a beautiful present for her mommy with all the other treasures she found.
