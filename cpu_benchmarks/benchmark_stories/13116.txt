One day, a child found a wrap. She was so excited about it. She had heard that popular people had wraps like these. She wanted to gain the wrap's power, so she put it on.
When she put on the wrap, the child suddenly felt very popular. Everywhere she went, grown-ups smiled at her and funny things happened to her. She felt so happy!
The wrap stayed with the child forever. Whenever she wanted to gain a new power, all she had to do was put on the wrap. Children everywhere wanted the wrap, because it made them feel popular, special and loved.
