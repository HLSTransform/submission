Once upon a time there was a mummy and a daddy who went to the park. Suddenly, mummy saw something mysterious. Daddy said it was a guard. They both let the guard know who they were. He said they could come in. When they went inside, the guard showed them a secret path. It was very dark and the ground was made of soft stones. It seemed like this path went on forever. The mummy and daddy were so excited! Eventually, the guard let them out of the mysterious path and they thanked him. Mummy and daddy were so happy they had found the secret path!
