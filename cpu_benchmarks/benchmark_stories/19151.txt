Once upon a time, in a small house, there lived a little girl named Amy. She loved to read books. One day, her mommy gave her a new book. It was a novel. Amy was very happy.
Amy wanted to introduce her new novel to her best friend, Tim. Tim lived next door. Tim was a mighty boy. He was very strong. He liked to play with Amy.
Amy and Tim sat under a big tree. They read the novel together. They laughed and smiled. The novel was fun. Amy and Tim had a great time. They loved their new novel. And they were the best of friends forever.
