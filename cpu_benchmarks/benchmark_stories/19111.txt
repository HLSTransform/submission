Once upon a time, there was a boy named Timmy. Timmy was a good boy who always listened to his parents. He was very obedient. One day, Timmy got a cold and had to go to the doctor. Timmy did not like going to the doctor because he was scared of shots. He hated shots.
When Timmy got to the doctor's office, he saw other kids waiting too. They looked scared too. But Timmy remembered that he had to be obedient and listen to the doctor. The doctor gave Timmy some medicine and told him to rest. Timmy felt better soon.
After that day, Timmy was not scared of going to the doctor anymore. He knew that the doctor was there to help him feel better. Timmy was happy that he was obedient and listened to the doctor.
