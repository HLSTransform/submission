Once upon a time, there was a mole who liked to explore wide open spaces. One day, the mole went on a big adventure and did not come back. Everyone was worried! Where had the mole gone?
The next day, the mole's friends were sad and scared. Then, they heard a noise coming from behind a bush. To their surprise, it was the mole! He had discovered a wide field and decided to explore it. 
Everyone laughed with joy and hugged the mole. But then something strange happened. The mole started to disappear! Everyone was shocked and started calling out his name.
Suddenly, the mole reappeared! He explained that he had found a magical tunnel that made him disappear and reappear. Everyone was happy and enjoyed their adventure together.
