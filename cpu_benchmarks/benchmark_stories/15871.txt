Once upon a time, there was a mama cow who wanted to succeed. She had no idea where to start, so she tried to think of a creative way to make something delicious. Suddenly, she remembered her favorite food was beef!
Mama cow excitedly mooed, “That’s it! I will make beef!” 
Mama cow went out to find grass, water and sun to make her beef. After a while, she had enough. Now, it was time to make the beef.
Mama cow used all her creative ideas to put the beef together. She tried different flavors and spices until it was perfect. 
Finally, mama cow looked at her beef and shouted, “I did it! I succeeded!”
Mama cow enjoyed her beef and was very proud of herself. With hard work and creativity, she had succeeded in making the most delicious beef ever!
