Once upon a time, a big volcano lived on a mountain. It was very hot and had red, orange, and yellow colors. One day, a little boy named Timmy come to see the volcano. He was very excited and happy. Timmy loved the colors and the sound of the volcano.
Suddenly, the volcano started to shake and make loud noises. Timmy got scared and started to run. But the volcano was fit and didn't want to hurt Timmy. So, it stopped shaking and became quiet. Timmy was very happy and came back to see the volcano again. From that day on, Timmy and the volcano became good friends.
