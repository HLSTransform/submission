Once upon a time, there was a boy named Timmy. Timmy loved music and he loved to play the piano. One day, Timmy's mommy took him to the music store to buy a piano. When they arrived, the store owner told them that there were no pianos available to buy. Timmy was sad.
But then, Timmy met a girl named Lily. Lily also loved music and she loved to sing. Timmy and Lily became friends and they played music together. One day, Timmy asked Lily if she wanted to marry him. Lily said yes! Timmy was so happy that he played a song on his imaginary piano for Lily. They lived happily ever after, playing music and singing together.
