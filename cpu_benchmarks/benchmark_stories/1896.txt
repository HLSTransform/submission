Once upon a time, there was a princess who was feeling very gloomy. She had a big challenge to succeed, but she wasn't sure if she could do it. She was scared it was too hard for her.
But her friends came to help her. They gave her kind words and said, “We believe in you, Princess!”
The Princess smiled and was feeling brave again. She was determined to succeed and she set out on her mission.
With the help and courage of her friends, she was able to do it. She had succeeded!
Everyone was so proud and they all cheered. The Princess was very happy, and her gloominess was gone.
