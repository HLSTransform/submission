Once upon a time, there was a big tower. It was very tall and had lots of windows. One day, a powerful wizard came to the tower. He wanted to set up his magic shop inside. 
The wizard had a big hat and a long beard. He had a wand that could do amazing things. He used his wand to set up his shop. He made sure everything was in the right place. 
Soon, people came from all over to see the wizard's magic shop. They were amazed by his powerful spells. The wizard was happy to help them all. And the tower became a very special place, full of magic and wonder.
