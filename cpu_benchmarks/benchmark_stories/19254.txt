Once upon a time, there was a rich man. He had enough money to buy a beautiful reef. On the reef was a lot of colorful coral and different kinds of fish, it was a very pretty sight.
Everyone who visited the reef was very impressed and they would applaud the man for his generosity. Even the fish and coral seemed happy, as they had a beautiful new home.
The wealthy man was very happy he could now share the reef with everyone, and lots of people came to see it each day. Soon, it became so popular that lots of people wanted to come to swim and explore.
The man was very proud his reef, every day he would visit it and see how happy it made people who visited it. When he saw how happy it made people, he couldn't help but applaud himself for creating a reef so wonderful.
