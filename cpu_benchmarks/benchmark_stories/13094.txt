Jack got ready and took off. He was running so fast! But then he heard his friend Jim yell out "Jack! You can do it!" Suddenly, Jack felt brave and he ran even faster.
Jack knew that he wasn't going to give up. He kept running and running until he crossed the line first.
Jack had won the race! Everyone cheered and cheered for him. Jack felt so proud and successful. He had worked hard and was brave enough to try his best. He was so excited to have won the race.
Jack had shown that he had what it took to be a winner. He was always going to remember this moment. He was always going to remember how brave he had been.
