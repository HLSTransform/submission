Once upon a time, there was a mysterious meeting. At the meeting was a small boy. He was very curious and he wanted to taste what was there.
The mysterious meeting had lots of yummy food. The boy saw cakes, chocolates, sweets and many other delicious things. He wanted to try them all!
He asked his mom if he could try some of the food. She said yes, so he tasted a little bit of everything. He was so happy! The boy loved all the different tastes.
The boy had a great time at the mysterious meeting. He tasted all the yummy food and laughed with his friends. And he never forgot that special and mysterious day.
