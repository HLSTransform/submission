Once upon a time, there was a boy who was very lucky. He loved to explore new things. One day, he went walking in the forest. As he was walking, he heard a noise. He was so surprised! He looked around him to see what it was - it was a big bear! The boy was so scared! He ran as fast as his legs would carry him. But he was very lucky - the bear didn't follow him! The boy made it back home safe and sound. He was lucky to have a safe adventure in the forest.
