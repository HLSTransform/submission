One day, there was a helpful dog in the hall. The dog was called Fluffy. Fluffy was so helpful, she loved to help people.
One day, there was something sticky on the floor. Fluffy saw it and she knew she had to help. She took out a cloth and wiped it up. Then the hall was clean!
The people in the hall were so happy. They thanked Fluffy for her help. Fluffy was proud of her work and she gave a happy bark.
Fluffy was a very helpful dog, and she had made the hall clean and tidy. Everyone was so happy to have her there.
