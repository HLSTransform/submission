One day, a little girl named Amy found a message on the ground. It was a fearful message. It said, "Help me! I am lost in the big woods." Amy wanted to solve the problem and help the person who wrote the message.
Amy asked her friend Tom, "Do you know who wrote this message?" Tom said, "I don't know, but we can look for clues in the big woods." They went to the big woods and started to look for the person who was lost.
As they walked, they saw a red hat on the ground. They remembered that their friend Sally had a red hat. They kept walking and found a toy that Sally liked to play with. Amy and Tom knew that Sally was the one who was lost. They found Sally crying under a big tree. She was happy to see her friends. Amy and Tom helped Sally find her way back home. They solved the problem and everyone was happy.
