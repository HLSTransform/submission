John looked around the church. He saw some friendly people. Everyone said hello and smiled. John liked seeing how friendly the church was.
John leaned against a bench and saw two people playing the piano. The music made him smile. He clapped his hands and started singing. Everyone laughed and looked at John.
The Pastor came out and said hello. He asked John if he wanted to come and join the church choir. John grinned and nodded.
John had a lot of fun singing in the choir. After the service, everyone said goodbye. They all said how happy they were to have John in the church. John was happy he had seen the friendly church.
