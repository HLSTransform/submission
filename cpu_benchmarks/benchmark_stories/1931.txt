Once there was a mother and a daughter. The daughter was very graceful and used to dance all the time. One day, the mother decided to take her daughter to a competition where they had to score the best points to win. The daughter was very excited to take part.
On the day of the competition, the mother and daughter did their best. The daughter danced gracefully and showed everyone her talent. After the scores were tallied, the daughter was expecting to win. But sadly, she had only scored the last place. 
The mother was so disappointed that she decided to go home. The daughter followed her, feeling sad. The mother had been hoping for the best, but it wasn't meant to be. 
The mother and daughter sadly went home and the daughter was never able to participate in the competition again. The story had sad end with mother and daughter disappointed.
