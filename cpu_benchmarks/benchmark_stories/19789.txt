Bob wanted to race. He invited his friends to take part in an impressive race. Everyone agreed and ran to the starting line.
Bob pressed the button and the race began. All the racers were so fast but Bob was the fastest. He passed the others and reached the finish line first.
The crowd was very excited. They shouted and clapped for Bob.
The organizers printed out a certificate for Bob. He was very proud to receive it.
Bob was the winner of the race. He was so happy that he wanted to race again and again.
