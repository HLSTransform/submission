Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She loved to play outside and explore the world around her. One day, she met a new friend named Sarah. Sarah was very kind and compassionate. 
Lily asked Sarah, "Can we be friends forever?" 
Sarah smiled and said, "Of course! I am so happy to have met you." 
As they played together, Lily became more and more like Sarah. She became more compassionate and kind to others. She realized that being a good friend was important and made her feel happy inside. 
From that day on, Lily and Sarah were the best of friends. They explored the world together, helping others along the way. They became known as the two compassionate girls who always had a smile on their faces.
