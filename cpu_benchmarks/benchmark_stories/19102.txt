Once upon a time, Santa's reindeer were getting ready for Christmas. They were happy and excited. But then, they saw a scary monster! The monster was big and loud. The reindeer were scared and didn't know what to do.
Suddenly, Santa rushed in. He saw the monster and knew what to do. He told the reindeer to stay back and he went to face the monster. Santa was brave and strong. He fought the monster and won!
The reindeer were so happy and proud of Santa. They knew they could always count on him. They all rushed back to the North Pole to get ready for Christmas. And that night, they all slept peacefully, knowing that Santa was there to protect them.
