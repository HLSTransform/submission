Once upon a time, there was a brave knight. He had a big sword that he used to fight bad guys. The sword was shiny and sharp.
One day, the knight went to a big castle where there was a big party. Everyone was happy and dancing. The knight felt happy too. He was very popular because he had saved many people before.
Suddenly, a bad guy came into the castle and started to hurt people. The knight took out his sword and fought the bad guy. He was very brave and strong. Finally, he defeated the bad guy and everyone cheered. The knight felt proud of himself and happy that he could help people.
