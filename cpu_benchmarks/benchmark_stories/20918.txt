Once upon a time, there was a brilliant nation. Everyone in the nation was kind and cheerful. They shared love and friendship, and the land remained peaceful.
One day, a special traveler from far away came to the nation. She had beautiful clothes and carried a shiny bag with her. When the traveler arrived, everyone gathered around her to welcome her.
The traveler smiled, and she shared her beautiful bag with the nation. Inside the bag were many special gifts, including a rainbow blanket and a very special heart-shaped box. Everyone was so happy to receive these amazing gifts.
The nation remained brilliant and full of love, and the traveler was always remembered with joy and appreciation.
