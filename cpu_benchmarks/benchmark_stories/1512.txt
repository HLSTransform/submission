Bob was a young boy. He had two parents and they loved him very much. They were married and Bob thought marriage was fine. Everyday, his parents spoiled him with toys and love. But one day, something changed.
Bob's parents started to fight a lot. He was so sad. He didn't understand why they were fighting. He wanted them to be happy like before. He hated seeing them fight.
One day, his father left. Bob missed him and felt very sad. But soon his mother told him that his father wouldn't be coming back. She said that they weren't married anymore.
Bob was so sad. He hated that his parents weren't married anymore. He missed his father but was glad they weren't fighting anymore. Still, he wished they could be married and happy like before.
