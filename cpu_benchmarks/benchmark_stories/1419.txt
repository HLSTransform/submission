Jimmy was 3 years old. He loved sports. He wanted to sign up for a sport, but he did not know which one.
He went to the park with his mom. He saw a lot of kids playing baseball. He liked the old ball and bat. He asked his mom if he could sign up for baseball.
His mom said yes, and the next day she took Jimmy to the park. The coach asked Jimmy to sign a paper. Jimmy was happy.
The coach showed Jimmy how to catch and hit the ball. He said he was a natural. Jimmy was very proud. 
Jimmy kept practicing and soon he was playing in the games. He kept playing longer and longer, and soon he was an expert at baseball. Jimmy loved being part of a team and playing a sport that he loved.
