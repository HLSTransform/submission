Once upon a time, there was a little girl who wanted a haircut. Her mum said she could get one if she was brave enough. The little girl was a bit scared, so the mum promised she could quit whenever she wanted.
The little girl bravely went to the salon. The hairdresser was very competitive and cut her hair like a star. After the haircut was finished, the little girl felt so proud of herself!
From then on, the little girl got a monthly haircut. Whenever it was time for the haircut, she didn't quit because she remembered how brave she was the first time.
