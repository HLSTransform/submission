Once upon a time, there was an ugly flute. No one wanted to play it. One day, a little girl heard the sad flute and decided to act. She took the flute and blew softly into it. Instead of a sad sound, the flute made a bright, beautiful noise. Everyone was so surprised! 
The little girl was so happy, she acted again and again. Everyone began to love the sound of the flute and came from far and wide to listen. It was the most beautiful music they had ever heard.
The little girl was so proud of what she had done. She had turned the ugly flute into a beautiful song. Everyone was happy and they lived happily ever after!
