Once there was a beautiful and graceful princess. She had a sparkling crown on her head, and everyone in the kingdom loved her. One day, the princess decided it was time for a feast. She invited everyone to her castle and invited them to eat. Everyone was eating yummy food and having a great time. But the princess was hungry too, so she grabbed a shiny crown and ate it! Everyone was so surprised that the princess ate a crown! But she just smiled and said, “A princess needs her crown to stay graceful!” Everyone laughed and the princess enjoyed her feast.
