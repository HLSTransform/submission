Once upon a time, there was a hero. He was very brave and strong. He saved people from danger. One day, he went to sleep in his bed. He had a dream that made him nervous. He woke up in the middle of the night and couldn't go back to sleep.
The next day, the hero went to save people from a fire. He was nervous because it was a big fire. He went inside the building to save a little girl. But the fire was too strong. The hero couldn't save the little girl and he got hurt. He was taken to the hospital, but he didn't make it. 
Everyone was sad that the hero had died. They remembered him as a brave and strong hero who saved many lives.
