Once upon a time, everywhere was dark. Then along came a special book. It was a magical book that shone very brightly.
The book made everyone happy. Then one day, something unexpected happened. The book split in two! It made a loud noise and the dark sky turned bright.
Everyone was so happy and surprised. They laughed and celebrated with the book. They hugged it, danced with it and shared it with each other. The book was a happy part of their lives. 
And the people never forgot the day the magical book split and chased away the dark.
