Once there was a brown uniform. It was very special. Everyone liked to wear it.
One day, the brown uniform disappeared! Nobody knew where it had gone. Everyone searched high and low, but it was nowhere to be found.
Suddenly, a young boy found the brown uniform behind a tree. He was very excited! He put it on and showed it to his friends. Everyone cheered and clapped.
The young boy was very proud. He had found the brown uniform and made everyone happy. The brown uniform never disappeared again.
