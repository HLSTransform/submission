Mummy and Daddy were getting ready to marry. They put on clean clothes and looked very nice. After they were dressed they went out the door.
Mummy said, "It's too hot outside! We can't get married now".
Daddy said, "Yes, we can! We have to stick to our plan".
Mummy and Daddy argued for a while, but in the end Daddy convinced Mummy to go ahead with their wedding. They held hands and went outside. Even though the sun was hot, they still got married! 
At the end of the ceremony, everyone cheered. They hugged and kissed each other. Mummy and Daddy were very happy!
