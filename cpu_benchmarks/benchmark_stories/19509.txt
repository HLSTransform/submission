Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Jane. One day, Jane got very sick. She felt like she had a cold and her stomach was hurting. 
So, her mommy took her to see the doctor. The doctor was a nice man who knew what to do. He listened to Jane and looked into her ears and throat. He said he had a cure for her. 
The doctor gave Jane some yummy medicine. And he told Jane and her mommy how to keep her from getting sick again. He said she needed to wash her hands because germs were dirty. 
Jane and her mommy made sure to follow the doctor's instructions. And soon, Jane was feeling all better. The doctor had provided the perfect medicine which made Jane feel much better.
