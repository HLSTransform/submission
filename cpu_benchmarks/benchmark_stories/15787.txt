Once upon a time there was a hero. His name was Jack and he was brave. Every day Jack would dream of being a hero. 
Jack's mommy told him, "If you want to be a hero, you have to be safe."
So Jack went on a big adventure. He wanted to prove he could be safe and be a hero. 
Jack saw a monster, He was scared and stopped walking. Then Jack heard a voice that said, "You don't have to be scared. Just keep walking and you'll be safe."
Jack walked on and soon he was safe. He felt brave and became a hero. 
The end.
