Once upon a time there was an old man. He had a naughty cat. His cat was always doing bad things. But today the old man found out a big mystery. He saw the cat lying in the sun. The old man wondered, "What is the cat doing?" He was very surprised to find out that the naughty cat was taking a nap! Suddenly the old man wanted to take a nap too. He lay down and had a long rest. And he was happy that the mystery was solved! The end.
