Anna and Ben are friends. They like to play together at school. They have different languages. Anna speaks English and Ben speaks Spanish. They learn some words from each other.
One day, they have a show and tell. Anna brings a book about animals. She shows the pictures and says the names in English and Spanish. Ben brings a toy car. He makes it go fast and says the colors in English and Spanish.
The other children listen and watch. They think Anna and Ben are interesting. They want to learn more words from them. They ask questions and try to repeat the words.
Anna and Ben are happy. They feel proud of their languages. They hug each other and say "thank you" and "gracias". They are good friends.
