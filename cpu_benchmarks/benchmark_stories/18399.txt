Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She loved to play with her toys. Her favorite toy was a teddy bear. One night, Lily was scared of the dark. She hugged her teddy bear tightly and wished she could settle down and sleep. Suddenly, she heard a noise. It was her mom coming to check on her. Her mom gave her a night light to help chase away the dark. Lily felt better and settled down with her toy. She hugged her teddy bear and fell asleep with a smile on her face.
