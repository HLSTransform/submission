Once, there was a girl who loved to jump. She was so happy, she couldn't stop smiling. Every day she would jump higher and higher. One day, the girl was so high she earned a medal! She was so happy she jumped higher and higher. Her face became pale, but she kept jumping. On her wall, she hung the medal so she could always see it. When she looked at it, she smiled and kept jumping. The girl loved her medal and kept jumping higher.
