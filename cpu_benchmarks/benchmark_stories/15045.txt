John was a lawyer and he worked hard everyday. He was so incredible at his job that he even had a special award. One day, he was walking through the park when he heard a loud noise. He looked around and saw that someone had scattered all of his papers. 
John was so angry that he ran over to the person who had made the noise. He demanded to know why they had scattered his papers. When the person didn't respond, John became angrier and started to hit the person. Suddenly a policeman appeared and saw what was happening.
The policeman arrested John for hitting the person. He was taken to court for his actions and was found guilty. He was sentenced to two years in prison for his crime and was taken away that day. John's incredible career as a lawyer was over.
