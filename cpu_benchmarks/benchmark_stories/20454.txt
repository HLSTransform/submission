He went so far and was having so much fun. He even made some new friends who were also on an adventure. They laughed and talked about what else they could do.
Will decided to try something new. He wanted to be famous! He wanted to be the first will to do this new and exciting thing. He was determined to be successful.
Will worked hard and finally did it. He made it to the top! Everyone celebrated his success and he became famous.
Will was so proud of himself and promised to continue on his adventures. He wanted to show everyone that anything was possible if you put your heart and mind to it. He was off again, on another adventure to be famous!
