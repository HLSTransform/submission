Once upon a time, there was a driver. He loved to drive his car around town. One day, something amazing happened. Suddenly, a bright light appeared in the sky. It was so big and beautiful! Everyone was amazed. Even the driver had to stop and look up at it. He wondered what it was. After a few moments, the light disappeared just as quickly as it had appeared. 
The driver was very curious. He decided to go find out where the light had come from. He drove around town and eventually he found it. It was an amazing castle! The driver was so excited! He parked his car and looked all around. He couldn’t believe his eyes. 
The driver had found a wonderful surprise. He couldn’t wait to tell his friends. He knew they would be amazed.
