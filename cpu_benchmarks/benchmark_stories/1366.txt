Once upon a time, there was a princess. She liked to imagine things. She imagined a big shiny silver castle. It was so tall, it touched the sky. She imagined walking in the castle, it felt safe and happy. Then she imagined finding a silver key in the castle. She picked it up and put it in her pocket. Later, she found a door in the castle and opened it. Behind the door, was a beautiful silver garden! She imagined the garden was safe and full of wonderful things. The princess smiled. She was so happy she could imagine wonderful places like this. The end.
