Once there was a guard who served the kingdom each day. He was a regular guard and always did what he was supposed to do. One day he had to serve something special. He was asked to guard the king's surprise! Everyone was so excited and happy. So the guard did his best to guard the surprise. He stood watch, checking all around until finally the surprise was revealed. It was a new castle that the king had built. Everyone cheered and said, "Thank you guard, for serving us today!"
