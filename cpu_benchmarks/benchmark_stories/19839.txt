Once there was a little boy who loved buying things. One day, he went to the store with his mom and dad. He saw so many delicious food. He asked his mom and dad to buy something. His dad said they would buy some words.
The little boy asked what were words. His mom said that words were like sentences. They could be used to talk to people. The little boy was very excited. He wanted to buy many words.
So they bought a big bag of words. The little boy was happy. He opened the bag and the words spilled out. The little boy liked the way the words sounded. He made delicious sentences with them.
The little boy enjoyed playing with the words. He gave them to his mom and dad and all his friends. Everyone thought the words were delicious too. They all laughed and had a wonderful time playing with the words.
