Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Timmy. Timmy liked to play with blocks. He had a lot of blocks that were different colors. One day, Timmy's mom gave him some new blocks. They were square and very colorful. Timmy was happy to have new blocks to play with.
Timmy started building with his new blocks. He made a tower that was very tall. Then, he made a castle that had four square walls. It was very colorful too. Timmy's mom came to see what he had made. She recognized that Timmy was very good at building with blocks.
Timmy was proud of his castle and he played with it every day. He liked the new square and colorful blocks that his mom gave him. He was happy to have so many blocks to play with.
