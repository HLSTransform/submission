Once upon a time there was a brave kitty. He liked to jump and explore. One day, the kitty saw something special. It was some colorful yarn! The kitty said, "Ooh! That looks fun!" He wanted to add the yarn to his collection. But the yarn was in a dangerous place. He had to be careful. The kitty made sure to be careful. He debated if he should try to add the yarn to his collection. He decided he had to try. He slowly moved close to the yarn. The kitty eventually got to the yarn and added it to his collection. The brave kitty was so proud. He had added another special thing to his collection. He was happy. The end.
