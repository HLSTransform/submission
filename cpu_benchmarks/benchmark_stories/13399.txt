Once upon a time, there was a kind boy named Timmy. He liked to explore the forest near his house. One day, he found a treasure hidden behind a big tree. He was very happy and wanted to show it to his friends. 
When Timmy went back to his house, he saw that his mom was talking to a new neighbor. The neighbor was very polite and said hello to Timmy. Timmy's mom asked him to say hello too. Timmy looked at the neighbor and said hello. 
After talking to the neighbor, Timmy showed his treasure to his friends. They were very excited and wanted to see it closer. Timmy let them look at it and they all thought it was very cool. Timmy was happy to have found the treasure and to have polite friends who liked to share his excitement.
