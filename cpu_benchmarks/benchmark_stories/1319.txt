Once upon a time, there was a delicate jail. It was a special place that kept people safe. But one day, the jail would not open. No matter how hard the people tried, they could not unlock it. 
Then a brave little boy came along. He had a secret plan. He knew just how to unlock the delicate jail. Everyone watched him in awe as he carefully worked his magic.
He used his special key to unlock the jail. A giant cheer went up as the door opened. Everyone was happy to be free and ran off to play. The little boy had unlocked the delicate jail!
