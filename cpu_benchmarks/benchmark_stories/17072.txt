Once upon a time, there was a dependable dog named Spot. Spot was a very good dog. He loved to help his family. One day, Spot's family needed him to deliver a ball to his little sister, Lily.
Spot picked up the ball with his mouth. He ran through the yard to find Lily. He was very fast! Spot saw Lily playing in the grass. He ran to her and dropped the ball at her feet. Lily was so happy!
Every day, Spot would deliver the ball to Lily. He was always dependable and made sure she had the ball to play with. Lily and Spot became the best of friends. They played together every day, and the family loved their dependable dog, Spot.
