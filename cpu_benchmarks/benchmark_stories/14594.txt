Once upon a time, there was a big, beautiful firework. It was so pretty that one day, a child decided to design it. The child took special care to make the firework look special. But then, one careless moment changed everything! The child got too close to the firework and there was a big BANG! The firework exploded and the child was very scared. Thankfully, no one was hurt and the child learned an important lesson - never be careless around firework. The end.
