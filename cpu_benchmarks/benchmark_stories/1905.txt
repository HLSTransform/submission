Once upon a time, there was a man called Mark. Mark liked to speed. He loved it! He would speed everywhere. 
One day, Mark decided to try and speed around the earth! He was so excited. He worked hard and he was patient. He asked a lot of questions and learned lots.
When he was ready, Mark began his mission to speed around the world. It was an amazing sight! He sped and sped and sped. Soon enough, the whole earth had been sped around!
Mark was so pleased with his work. He had done it! He had speeded around the earth. Everyone was cheering and cheering. 
They all thanked him for being so patient and determined. He had been so brave and never gave up. 
Mark had done it! He had sped around the earth.
