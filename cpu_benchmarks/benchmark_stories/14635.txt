Once upon a time there was a little boy called Joe. Joe was very sad and wanted to be happy. One day, he got a special ticket. The ticket was very powerful and it could make him heal. 
Joe was so excited, he ran near the ticket and when he got close, the ticket started to glow. Joe was amazed and he felt the power of the ticket. Suddenly, he felt different - he felt his sadness disappearing and he was feeling happy. 
Every day Joe kept the ticket close to him and it kept healing him. His friends had noticed a difference in him and they were happy. Joe was really happy - he was feeling better and better. 
He was so thankful for the powerful ticket. Whenever he felt sad, he would hold the ticket close to him and it would make him feel better. Joe was so glad and he was so happy he got the special ticket. 
The End.
