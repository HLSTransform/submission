Lucy loved to jog. Every day she would jog around the park. One sunny day, Lucy decided to go for a jog. As she jogged, she noticed a math book on the ground. She had always wanted to learn more about math. She picked it up and started to read.
Lucy was so alert as she read the math book. She was attentive and eager to learn. She jogged around the park and memorized all the new math facts. Soon, she was able to answer all the math questions in the book with ease.
Lucy was so proud of herself. She had used jogging and the math book to learn a lot of new math facts. She smiled widely, happy that her hard work had paid off. Lucy had become a master of math.
