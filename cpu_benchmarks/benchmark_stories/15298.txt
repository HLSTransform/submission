Once upon a time, there was a purple surprise. It was wrapped up in a box. But what was inside? Everybody was excited to find out.
Suddenly, a little girl had an idea! She opened the box and there was a purple cat inside! Everyone was so surprised. The cat was wearing a sparkly purple bow.
The little girl was so happy with the surprise. She loved playing with the cat. She even named it Purple! Everywhere they went, they were sure to bring lots of surprises.
The end.
