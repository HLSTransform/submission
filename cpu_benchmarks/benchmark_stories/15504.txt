The sun was hot. It was march. There were lots of people in the march. They wanted to prevent bad things from happening.
The march was made up of people from many different places. Some were rich, some had less money. All of the people wanted to be free.
The people wanted to prevent bad things from happening to them and to everyone else. They wanted to make sure everyone was safe and no one got hurt.
The march was big. All the people sang and chanted together. They wanted to make sure they were heard. Everyone marched until they got to the end. 
When they got there, they cheered and hugged each other. They were glad they had been able to come together. They knew they had done a good thing to prevent bad things from happening.
