Once there was a little girl called Lucy. She was very lucky. Today she wanted to go on a search. She grabbed her coat and set off. In her search, she found something amazing! Lucy saw a big rock that was sparkling in the sun. She went closer to have a better look and when she touched the rock, it suddenly struck and the ground started shaking. She was so excited to find such a special treasure. She smiled and hugged the rock tightly. She had been so lucky today!
