Once upon a time, there was a fisherman. He liked to catch fish in a big pond. The pond had many fish, and the fisherman was happy.
One day, the fisherman saw a hidden spot in the pond. He kneeled down by the water and looked at the fish. The fish were big and colorful. He wanted to catch them.
The fisherman tried to catch the fish in the hidden spot. He was very happy when he caught them. He took the fish home to show his family. They all smiled and clapped. The fisherman had a good day.
